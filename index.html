About us

DHC is an organisation formed in 2004 by local GPs to provide high quality, local and timely outpatient appointments initially for patients of the Dorking practices. In recent years we have widened our mission to provide services for patients throughout Surrey and Sussex. Outpatient services are available at Dorking Hospital and at Holmhurst Medical Centre, Redhill.
